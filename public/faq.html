FAQ

1 What is AstroShift? +

AstroShift is a lightning-fast cryptocurrency exchange platform that allows you to:

  • Exchange one cryptocurrency for another instantly
  • Buy crypto using Cash App, Zelle, or PayPal
  • Get the best rates with transparent fees

We're designed to make crypto accessible to everyone, whether you're a seasoned trader or just getting started.

2 Why should I choose AstroShift? +

Simple & Fast: No complicated sign-ups or lengthy verification processes. Start exchanging in seconds.

Multiple Payment Options: We accept Cash App, Zelle, and PayPal for fiat-to-crypto purchases.

Transparent Fees: Our fees are clearly displayed before you confirm any exchange. No hidden charges.

24/7 Support: Our team is available around the clock to help with any questions.

3 Is AstroShift safe to use? +

Yes! Security is our top priority:

  • No custody: We don't hold your funds. Crypto is sent directly to your wallet.
  • Encrypted connections: All data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.
  • Instant processing: Funds aren't stored on our platform, minimizing risk.

No account is required to make an exchange! Simply:

However, if you'd like to track your orders and receive email notifications, you can optionally sign up with just your email address.

1 What is the difference between fixed and float rates? +

Fixed Rate (1.0% fee):

  • The rate you see is the rate you get
  • Protected from market volatility during the exchange
  • Best for larger transactions or volatile markets

Float Rate (0.5% fee):

  • Lower fee but rate may change slightly
  • Final amount determined when your transaction confirms
  • Could be higher or lower depending on market movement
2 What are AstroShift's fees? +

Crypto-to-Crypto:

  • Fixed rate: 1.0%
  • Float rate: 0.5%

Fiat-to-Crypto (Cash App, Zelle, PayPal):

  • Exchange fee: 1.0%
  • Fiat processing fee: 5.5%
  • Total: 6.5%

All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm your exchange.

3 How long does an exchange take? +

Crypto-to-Crypto: Usually 5-30 minutes, depending on:

  • Network congestion
  • Number of confirmations required
  • The specific cryptocurrencies involved

Fiat-to-Crypto: After your payment is verified (usually within 5-15 minutes), the crypto exchange typically completes within 10-30 minutes.

4 Is there a minimum or maximum exchange amount? +

Minimum amounts:

  • Fiat purchases: $5.00 minimum
  • Crypto exchanges: Varies by currency (shown during exchange)

Maximum amounts:

  • Standard exchanges have no maximum
  • For very large amounts, contact support for the best rates
5 Why is my wallet address recognized as invalid? +

This usually happens because:

  • Wrong network: Make sure you're using the correct address format for the cryptocurrency you're receiving
  • Copy/paste error: Double-check that you copied the entire address without extra spaces
  • Incompatible address: Some exchanges generate addresses that aren't compatible with direct transfers

If you're sure your address is correct, please contact our support team.

1 How can I track my order? +

There are several ways to track your order:

  • Order page: After creating an exchange, you'll be taken to an order status page. Bookmark this page!
  • Track Order: Use the "Track Order" link in the navigation and enter your Order ID
  • Email notifications: If you provided your email, you'll receive updates on your order status
2 What should I do if my order expired? +

If your order expired before you sent the funds:

  • Simply create a new order with the same details
  • The rate may have changed since your original order

If you sent funds to an expired order:

  • Contact our support team immediately with your Order ID
  • We'll help recover your funds or complete the exchange manually
3 I accidentally closed the page. What should I do? +

Don't worry! Your order is still processing. Here's what to do:

  • Check your email for the order confirmation (if you provided one)
  • Go to "Track Order" and enter your Order ID
  • Check your browser history for the order page URL

If you can't find your Order ID, contact support with your wallet address and approximate time of the transaction.

4 Why is my order taking so long? +

Exchange times can vary due to:

  • Network congestion: High traffic on blockchain networks can slow confirmations
  • Low transaction fees: If you sent crypto with a low fee, it may take longer to confirm
  • Required confirmations: Some currencies require more confirmations for security

Most exchanges complete within 30 minutes. If it's been longer, please contact support.

1 How do Cash App payments work? +

After creating your order:

  • You'll see our Cash App $cashtag
  • Open your Cash App and send the exact amount shown
  • Enter the Cash App transaction ID on our site
  • We verify the payment and send your crypto

Important: Send the exact amount shown. Sending more or less may delay your order.

2 How do Zelle payments work? +

After creating your order:

  • You'll see our Zelle email address
  • Send the exact amount via your bank's Zelle feature
  • Enter the Zelle confirmation number on our site
  • We verify the payment and send your crypto

Note: Zelle payments are usually instant, but verification may take a few minutes.

3 How do PayPal payments work? +

After creating your order:

  • You'll see our PayPal email address
  • Send the exact amount as "Friends & Family"
  • Enter the PayPal transaction ID on our site
  • We verify the payment and send your crypto

Important: Always send as "Friends & Family" to avoid delays. "Goods & Services" payments may be refunded.

4 Why is there a $5 minimum for fiat payments? +

The $5 minimum exists because:

  • Payment processing has fixed costs regardless of amount
  • Blockchain transaction fees make small amounts impractical
  • It ensures you receive a meaningful amount of crypto
5 What if I sent the wrong amount? +

If you sent less: Contact support. We can either refund you or adjust your order.

If you sent more: Contact support. We'll either send the extra crypto or refund the difference.

Always contact us with your Order ID for the fastest resolution.
