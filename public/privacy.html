Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

1. Information Collection

We collect minimal information necessary to process your exchange. This typically includes your wallet address and transaction hash. We do not require account registration for basic exchanges.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of processing your transaction and providing customer support if needed.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our site, such as remembering your selected currency pair. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services (such as FixedFloat) to facilitate cryptocurrency exchanges. Please review their privacy policies as well.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.
